### Target Voice:
- **70% Clear and Informative** - Professional, straightforward, educational

## Tone Checklist for New Content

Before publishing any new content, verify:

- [ ] Is it 70% clear and informative?
- [ ] Is it 30% premium and minimal (not flashy)?
- [ ] Does it avoid confusing jargon?
- [ ] Does it make realistic claims only?
- [ ] Does it build trust through transparency?
- [ ] Does it emphasize human guidance + modern tools?
- [ ] Does it avoid false promises about admissions/visas?

---

## PRD-Compliant Language Examples

### ✅ Good Examples:
- "We guide you through the process" (not "We guarantee admission")
- "95% of our students report satisfaction" (not "100% success rate")
- "Clear, step-by-step process" (not "Easy and fast")
- "Expert human guidance" (not "AI-powered automation")

### ❌ Avoid:
- "Guaranteed admission to top universities"
- "Fast-track visa approval"
- "Secret insider connections"
- "Revolutionary AI technology"
- "100% success rate"
