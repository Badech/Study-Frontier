# Study Frontier — Full Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product overview

**Product name:** Study Frontier
**Type:** Premium educational consulting platform
**Launch positioning:** USA-first educational consulting agency for Moroccan students
**Long-term vision:** Multi-country, tech-enabled educational consultancy platform with a premium public brand and a structured private client experience

Study Frontier is a premium, honest, and tech-enabled educational consulting platform that helps Moroccan students pursue their studies in the USA through a clear, structured process.

The product is not just a marketing website. It is a full platform with 3 layers:

1. **Public website** for trust, lead generation, and partner credibility
2. **Student/client portal** for documents, progress tracking, appointments, payments, and visa workflow
3. **Admin dashboard** for lead management, student operations, workflow control, payments, and reporting

---

## 2. Product vision

Build the most **organized, honest, and premium digital experience** for Moroccan students pursuing studies in the USA.

Study Frontier should feel:

- **trustworthy first**
- **step-by-step second**
- **premium and professional third**
- **specifically relevant to Moroccan students fourth**

Core brand promise:

## **Clear Process. No Confusion.**

## 21. DS-160 module

This is an advanced guided workflow inside the platform.

Students must be able to fill all DS-160 questions inside the dashboard and submit them for review.

### DS-160 workflow

1. Student fills full DS-160 questionnaire inside the portal
2. System autosaves progress
3. Student submits to Study Frontier for review
4. Admin reviews for issues/inconsistencies
5. Student corrects if needed
6. Case becomes “Ready for Final DS-160 Processing”

### Important product decision

The platform does **not** directly submit to the official U.S. government system in initial versions.

It is an intake + review + readiness workflow.

## 23. Payment system

## Temporary launch payment model

Use **PayPal Business Invoices** with admin-reviewed payment requests.

### Why

You already have a US PayPal Business account and students can pay the invoice flow.

### Temporary payment flow

1. student becomes ready for payment
2. platform creates payment request record
3. admin reviews/approves
4. admin sends PayPal invoice
5. system tracks payment state manually/semi-automatically

## Long-term payment architecture

The platform must be built so payment providers can be swapped later.

Planned future provider:

- Moroccan business gateway after registration
